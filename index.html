MoodPrism

A mood analyzing application.

Begin

Description

Mental fatigue and state of mind are factors that are usually devalued by users but often can lead to human failures and even be the cause of some health issues and a decreased quality of life. In order to prevent this, and invest in the mental health of workers, we present our project, MoodPrism, which is a distributed system that will detect and monitor users mental fatigue and state of mind. In our project, we will focus on mental fatigue, which is a very important factor for the performance of users, and can be monitored in real time, while they are working. Our system is directed to users that work on a computer, with information being captured by the keyboard and mouse, that will later be processed and reveal the current mood of the workers using it.
