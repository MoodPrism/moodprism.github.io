How will we get our results:

The results will be presented after we process the collected data, such as the frequency with which a person presses a certain key, such as Espace, which usually indicates frustration. It will also be collected the frequency with which the user moves the mouse and clicks on its buttons, with all this data being subsequently processed, providing the result that is more likely to be the correct one, because for most occasions, there are more than une acceptable result.
